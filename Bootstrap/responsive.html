Lesson notes

Bootstrap is mobile-first: combine the 12-column grid, breakpoints, and utilities to make layouts that scale from phones to desktops.

  • Navbar: navbar-expand-md to collapse into a hamburger on small screens
  • Utilities: w-100 w-md-auto, spacing p-3 p-md-4, display d-none d-md-block
  • Media: img-fluid for images, .ratio for responsive iframes/videos
  • Breakpoints: xs, sm, md, lg, xl, xxl
