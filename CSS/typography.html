Typography

Typography in CSS is the styling of text, it covers fonts, sizes, spacing, and alignment to make content clear, readable, and visually appealing.

Lesson notes

Typography controls how text looks and reads on a page — choosing fonts, sizes, spacing, and alignment to create clear hierarchy and improve readability.

  • font-family, font-size, font-weight — choose appropriate fonts and sizes.
  • line-height, letter & word spacing — improve readability and rhythm.
  • Use web fonts (Google Fonts or @font-face) for consistent branding.
  • Responsive typography with rem, clamp(), and media queries.
  • Accessibility: good contrast, readable sizes, and scalable text for different users.
